Menna Balbaa

CV / Mennatallah.Balbaa@lmu.de / Scholar

I am a postdoctoral researcher and assistant lecturer at the Institute for Capital Markets and Corporate Finance at LMU Munich. I received a Ph.D. in Finance in 2025, specializing in sustainable finance and impact investing. I am passionate about economic development and integrating ESG factors into financial decision-making.

Research

My work focuses on exploring drivers for sustainable investments and inferring policy recommendations to promote these investments in both developed and developing economies.

Teaching

At LMU Munich, I have taught and supervised students in finance-related courses and seminars. I enjoy mentoring and guiding students through the development of their theses and research projects. My teaching spans topics such as sustainable finance, impact investing, and corporate valuation.
