Menna Balbaa

Research / Teaching

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher and Assistant Lecturer at the Institute for Capital Markets and Corporate Finance at LMU Munich. I received my Ph.D. in Finance in 2025, specializing in sustainable finance and impact investing. I am passionate about economic development and integrating ESG factors into financial decision-making.

Research

My work focuses on exploring drivers for sustainable investments and inferring policy recommendations to promote these investments in both developed and developing economies.

  • Balbaa, M., Jiao, P., Witt, M., & Zhu, X. (2024). SRI or Diversify? Working Paper.
    Presented at: Seminar Maastricht University, and Osaka University, Experimental Finance Conference 2024, Boulder Summer Conference on Consumer Financial Decision Making 2024.

  • Balbaa, M., Dong, B., Jiao, P., & Worch, M. (2024). Norms Make Investors Socially Responsible. Working Paper.
    Presented at: Seminar Maastricht University, LMU Munich, and Glasgow University, Experimental Finance Conference 2023, Experimental Sustainable Finance Symposium Radboud University 2024, European Sustainable Finance PhD Workshop 2024, Boulder Summer Conference on Consumer Financial Decision Making 2024.

  • Balbaa, M., & Worch, M. (2024). Sustainability, Prices, and Emotions. Working Paper.
    Presented at: Seminar Maastricht University, CESS Nuffield Laboratory, Oxford, and LMU Munich, Experimental Finance Conference 2023, Global Research Alliance for Sustainable Finance and Investment (GRASFI) Conference at Yale University 2023, GRASFI PhD Workshop 2023, European Conference of the Financial Management Association 2024.

Teaching

As an Assistant Lecturer, I have taught and supervised students in finance courses and seminars. I enjoy mentoring and guiding students through the development of their theses and research projects, having supervised more than 50 bachelor and master theses to date. My teaching spans topics such as risk management, behavioral finance, and corporate finance.

  • Seminar: Selected Topics in Finance (WS 20/21 – WS 24/25)

  • SQ: Start with Business Planning (SS 21, SS 22, SS 23)
    Undergraduate tutorial on developing a business plan
  • Behavioral Finance (WS 22/23 – WS 24/25)

  • Corporate Finance (SS 24)
